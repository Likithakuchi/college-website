Steps

Admission Process Timeline

Understand the step-by-step roadmap to enroll in our technical courses.

Step 1: Online Application

Fill up Application Form

Fill in academic history, select desired stream, and pay a $50 registration fee via the secure online gateway below. Form draft saves automatically.

Step 2: Entrance Examination

Take Entrance Evaluation

Shortlisted candidates will be notified to take the online LIKITHA-SAT test. Exemption is given for candidates with national ranks (SAT/GATE/JEE).

Step 3: Counseling & Interview

Interactive Counseling Board

Shortlisted applicants undergo academic counselling and personality checks with the advisory board to finalize major allocations.

Step 4: Document Verification

Secure Admissions & Register

Submit academic mark sheets, recommendation letters, health certificates, and identification proof. Download brochure to view the complete list.

Required Documents

Documents Checklist

Ensure you have all scan files ready before submitting the admission form.

  • High School / 10th Grade Graduation Certificate
  • Higher Secondary / 12th Grade Mark Sheet (or equivalent transcript)
  • SAT, GATE, JEE entrance score card (if applicable)
  • Character and Migration certificate from last attended institution
  • Three recent passport-sized color photographs
  • Government issued Identity Proof (Passport, Driving License, National ID card)
