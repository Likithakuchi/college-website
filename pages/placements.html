Corporate Liaison

Corporate Relations & Placement Cell

Our Corporate Liaison Board acts as a direct bridge between major recruiters and student bodies. Operating 365 days a year, the board conducts campus recruitment drives, soft skills training camps, and mock assessment mock drills.

Pre-placement training models are integrated right into the academic curriculum starting from pre-final year semesters, preparing candidates for complex algorithms and corporate interview rounds.

Real-World Exposure

Paid Internships & Industry Ties

Before graduating, students acquire industry project credits via our mandate semester-long internship program. Leading hardware/software firms recruit pre-final year candidates for onsite internships.

In the 2025-26 cycle, over 85% of our B.Tech students secured pre-placement offers (PPOs) directly from their internships, with average student stipends hovering around $450/month.
