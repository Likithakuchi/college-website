About Our Institution

A journey of academic excellence, research advancements, and career launches over two decades.

Since 2005

Our History

Likitha University was founded in 2005 by the Likitha Educational Trust with the core goal of bridging the gap between industry needs and technical academic curriculum in the region.

Starting with just two departments (Computer Science and Electronics) and a modest cohort of 120 students, Likitha has grown dynamically into a multidisciplinary university campus offering engineering, management, and research courses to over 5,000+ students.

Over the years, we have forged active collaborations with companies like TCS, Infosys, and Qualcomm, created a state-of-the-art incubation and startup cell, and built scientific laboratories where students solve global engineering issues.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a premier university of technical education and scientific research, fostering ethical leaders who innovate and transform industries.

Our Mission

  • To provide rigorous, industry-aligned curricula that build robust problem-solving competencies.
  • To promote state-of-the-art laboratory research that addresses contemporary real-world challenges.
  • To cultivate high ethical standards, entrepreneurial mindsets, and corporate collaboration skills.
