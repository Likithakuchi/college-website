Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers regarding admissions criteria, hostel facilities, placements, and scholarship distributions.

How can I apply for undergraduate engineering courses?
You can apply online by visiting our Admissions page, filling up the Application Form, uploading academic transcripts, and submitting the entry test score. Registration portal handles documents directly.
Is hostel accommodation available for national and international students?
Yes, Likitha hosts multiple multi-tier hostel buildings with single, double, and quad rooms equipped with central cooling, Wi-Fi networks, mess facilities, indoor game rooms, and 24x7 security personnel.
What are the eligibility criteria for merit-based scholarships?
Scholarships are awarded based on Board Exams scores or state/national level entrance examinations rank. Candidates with GPA > 3.8 or > 95% receive 50% tuition fee waiver automatically.
What kind of internship opportunities does placement cell facilitate?
The corporate relations division facilitates industry-integrated internships during the pre-final year. Our students regularly get paid internships in Microsoft, Qualcomm, TCS, and Wipro with average stipend exceeding $500/month.
When is the last date to register and pay admission fees?
For Fall Session 2026-27, online registration closes on July 31, 2026. Document verification and seat confirmation fee payment must be done on or before August 10, 2026.
